Plain English. No nonsense.

Who we are

Stellar Lift Blog (“we”, “us”) is available at blog.stellarliftapp.com.

  • Basic usage information (for example: pages viewed, approximate device/browser info, and general region) to understand what content is useful.

Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep the site working and to measure and improve content. We also use them for advertising.

If you are in the EEA/UK, we display a consent message and store your choices. You can also control cookies in your browser settings.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display ads on this site.

  • AdSense may use cookies or device identifiers to show ads, measure performance, and limit repeated ads.
  • Depending on your region and choices, ads may be personalized or non-personalized.
  • If consent is required in your region (e.g. EEA/UK), AdSense will respect your selection made in the consent message.

Third-party services

Some features are provided by third parties. These parties may process data under their own policies:

  • Google (AdSense) – advertising, reporting, fraud prevention.
  • GitHub Pages – hosting and delivery of site content.

Your choices

  • You can manage your ad and cookie choices via the consent message (where shown) and your browser settings.
  • You can also review and adjust Google’s advertising preferences in your Google account settings.

Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed to operate the site, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Children

This site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
