Cookies & Ads

What we use, why we use it, and how you control it.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device. They help sites work correctly and help measure what content performs best.

What we use cookies for

  • Site functionality – basic features and stability.
  • Measurement – understanding what pages people read and what to improve.
  • Advertising – showing ads and measuring ad performance (Google AdSense).

Consent (EEA/UK)

If you are in the EEA/UK, we show a consent message. Your choice determines whether ads can be personalized and what measurement is allowed.

Personalized vs non-personalized ads

  • Personalized ads may use cookies or identifiers to show more relevant ads.
  • Non-personalized ads do not use personalization, but may still use limited signals for fraud prevention and frequency capping.

How to control cookies

  • Use the consent message (where shown) to change your choice.
  • Use your browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • If you use Google services, you can manage ad personalization in your Google account settings.
